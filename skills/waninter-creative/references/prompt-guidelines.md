# Prompt Guidelines

## Images

Include:

- Main subject
- Environment or background
- Visual style
- Lighting
- Composition
- Camera/lens if relevant
- Color palette
- Aspect ratio or intended use

Example:

```text
A luxury perfume bottle on wet black stone, premium product photography, soft studio lighting, shallow depth of field, gold reflections, dramatic shadows, 16:9
```

## Videos

Include:

- Subject
- Action or transformation over time
- Camera movement
- Scene progression
- Motion intensity
- Duration
- Aspect ratio
- Visual style

Example:

```text
A cinematic drone shot flying forward over a futuristic coastal city at sunset, warm golden light, gentle atmospheric haze, smooth slow camera movement, realistic, 5 seconds, 16:9
```

## Editing images

Be specific about what changes and what must remain unchanged:

```text
Remove the background and make it transparent. Keep the product shape, reflections, and label text unchanged. Clean edges, no extra objects.
```
